GEMSBOK

the gemsbok or gemsbuck(oryx gazella) is a large antelope in the arid region of southern africa, such as kalahari desert

the gemsbok is depicted on the coat of arms of Namibia, where number of species is estimated at 373,000 individuals.

the name"gemsbok" in english isis derived from afrikaans gemsbokke and Dutch name of the male chamois, gemsbok. although some superficial similarities are noticed, chamois and the oryx are not closely related

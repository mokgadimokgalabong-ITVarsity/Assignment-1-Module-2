LION

The lion(Panthea lio)is a large cat of the genus panthera native to Africa and India. it has a muscular, broad-chested body,short,rounded head, round ears, and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail. it is a sexually dimorphobic, adult male lions are larger than females and have a prominent mane.
